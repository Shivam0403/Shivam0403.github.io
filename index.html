Computer Club

About Us

Computer club is the technical club of the computer science department of Sgsits college indore. The club was established in january 2018 under Ms Priyanka bamne as the mentor of the club , and got its pace subsequently.

We as a whole team work with each other for the betterment of our department and future of every student. We always encourage students to explore fields and enhance their skills. We are always open to new technologies and ideas. We believe in constant learning and so we come up with new ways and innovative ideas of learning to keep interest of students, whether itâ€™s their technical skills or soft skills.
Our club has organised many activities in the past few years which have proved beneficial and students have learned alot.

The club acts as a support system for the students to get work experience through practical projects and complete their technical part of personality. It aims at providing work experience starting right from the 1st year, uplifting the technological standards of students , and bringing in the culture of work while you play for their betterment . Overall it is a great work space for students to learn and implement.

What we do ?

Motive


The motive of the club is to support the students of the department in all the areas like academics , improving their personalities, preparing them to face interviews, giving the knowledge about backhand languages and much more.

Activities


We conduct various events like workshop,coding competition and hackathons to keep the competitive spirit going in each of its members . We have something for everyone whether you want to learn or participate.

Buddy
